Examples

Ceres.js is a versatile optimization library that can handle a wide range of problems and is well-suited for solving large and complicated optimization problems. The fact that it is a mature and performant library, with a wealth of features, makes it an attractive option for developers looking to solve optimization problems in JavaScript. Additionally, the fact that it is an open-source library that is based on the popular Ceres Solver library means that it has a strong community of users and contributors who can offer support and contribute to its development.
